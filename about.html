Jake Schneider

President and CEO, Schneider Econoimics


Jake W. Schneider is a Master in Public Administration in International Development candidate at the Harvard Kennedy School (2020) where he concentrates on the intersection of economics, public policy and international development. He currently serves as Guest Lecturer at the Thayer School of Engineering at Dartmouth College where he presents a lecture series on Big Data, Data Science and improvements international healthcare. Prior to his matriculation at the Kennedy School, Mr. Schneider worked at the Brookings Institution where he researched and authored several reports on how to increase investment in global health. Previously, Mr. Schneider worked as a research assistant directly for Former Federal Reserve Chairman Alan Greenspan at Greenspan Associates, LLC and as a research intern in the Office of Economic Policy at the U.S. Treasury Department. Mr. Schneider holds a B.A. magna cum laude with honors from the College of William & Mary in Virginia (2015).
